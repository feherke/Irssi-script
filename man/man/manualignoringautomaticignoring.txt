10.2 %9Automatic ignoring%9

Irssi can automatically set ignores for people who flood you. Currently you can
autoignore %9MSGS%9, %9NOTICES%9, %9CTCPS%9 and %9PUBLIC%9. Actions are placed to either %9MSGS%9
or %9PUBLIC%9. See section 3.3 for definition of the flood.

/%wSET%N %cautoignore_time%N <seconds> specifies how long to ignore the user.

/%wSET%N %cautoignore_levels%N <levels> specifies what levels to ignore automatically,
default is to ignore only CTCPS.
